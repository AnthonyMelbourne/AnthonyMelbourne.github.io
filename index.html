About Me

"Everyday day is a school day" is a phrase I was taught a long time ago while working in the hospitality industry and it's something I carry with me to this day, reminding myself that it's never too late to learn or try something new. Even if it forces me out of my comfort zone.

During the Covid lockdowns beginning in March 2020 I started learning basic HTML5, CSS & JavaScript along with a familiarity with Jquery, Bootstrap & Paper.JS. Gradually building the confidance to learn React.JS, Vue.JS & Tailwind CSS with aspirations of becoming a Front End Web Developer.
